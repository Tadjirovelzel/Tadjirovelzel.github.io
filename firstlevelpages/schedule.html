Instruction Schedule

Almost every machine requires access in toolsquare. There are regular instructions during which you can learn how the machines work and acquire access in toolsquare. Instructions are generally only given if three or more students sign up. Make sure you let us know when you want to attend an instruction by filling in this google form and we will confirm your reservation or contact you to reschedule.

Instructions

This google calendar shows the reservations of machines made by users. On the bottom left of the page, it's possible to click the google calendar button to add this calendar to your google account. In order to make your own reservations, you will have to request access from one of the staff members. After obtaining access, you will be able to make your own reservations in the google calendar app or on the google calendar website.
