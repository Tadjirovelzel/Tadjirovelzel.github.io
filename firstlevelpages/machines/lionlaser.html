Lion Laser M1300

The Lion Laser M1300 is the most used laser in the workplace. It is perfect for making large cuts and has a great build volume. It is best suited for cutting materials up to 12mm thick.
The laser requires access in toolsquare which can be obtained during the regular instructions.
For a machine that is specialised in accurate cuts and engraving small surfaces, check out the Epilog Zing 16. Please note that this machine is not fit for cutting metal.

Spec Summary

The following table sums up the specifications relevant for the use of the Lion M1300 in our workspace.

Work area 1250 x 900 mm
Laserhead HP 2025 industrial cutting head with coaxial gas flow and interchangeable nozzles Gas assist to 5 bar
Type of laser 80W CO2-laser / 100W CO2-laser
Wavelength 10640 nm
Pulse output 80W / 100W
Required Access Basic toolsquare access
